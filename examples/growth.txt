# Latent Growth Curve Model
# Linear growth over four time points
#
# Latent factors:
#   i — intercept factor (all loadings fixed to 1)
#   s — slope factor     (loadings fixed to 0, 1, 2, 3 to represent time)
#
# The intercept factor captures the starting level;
# the slope factor captures the rate of linear change.
# Their covariance models individual differences in trajectories.

# Measurement model (fixed loadings define the growth shape)
i =~ 1*y1 + 1*y2 + 1*y3 + 1*y4
s =~ 0*y1 + 1*y2 + 2*y3 + 3*y4

# Factor (co)variances — freely estimated
i ~~ i
s ~~ s
i ~~ s

# Intercepts of the latent factors (mean intercept and mean slope)
i ~ 1
s ~ 1

# Residual variances of the observed variables — freely estimated
y1 ~~ y1
y2 ~~ y2
y3 ~~ y3
y4 ~~ y4

# Constrain residual means to zero (standard growth model assumption)
y1 ~ 0*1
y2 ~ 0*1
y3 ~ 0*1
y4 ~ 0*1
